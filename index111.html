Calvin Klein 

  
		American fashion designer, 
	in 1968 founded his own company Calvin Klein Inc.
	and who owned it until 2003. 
	   Klein married Jayne Centre,
	       a textile designer, in 1964.
                They divorced in 1974.

Versace

   
		Italian luxury fashion company 
	            and trade name founded by Gianni
	             Versace in 1978.
	            The main collection of the brand 
	            is Versace,  
		  which produces upmarket Italian-made 
			 ready-to-wear and leather
			  accessories,  while other 
			  diffusion lines are Versace
			  Collection (mainly in the US), 
			  Versus Versace and Versace Jeans.

Chanel

 
   French privately held company 
	 owned by Alain Wertheimer and Gérard Wertheimer, 
	 grandsons of Pierre Wertheimer, 
	            who was an early business partner 
                    of the couturière Gabrielle Bonheur Chanel.

Christian Dior

 
     European luxury goods company controlled 
         and chaired by French businessman 
                   Bernard Arnault, who also heads LVMH, 
                           the world's largest luxury group.
               The company was founded in 1946 by designer 
                                                   Christian Dior.

D&G

 
 Italian luxury fashion house 
   founded in 1985 in Legnano by 
     Italian designers Domenico Dolce and Stefano Gabbana.
       The two met in Milan in 1980 and 
          designed for the same fashion house. 
     In 1982, they established a designer 
         consulting studio; in time it grew to become 
       "Dolce & Gabbana".
